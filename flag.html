Our motto, “Our Sun is Our Soul” means we cherish our Sun.
There is happiness where the Sun shines, and our souls are full of the
satisfaction of our freedom.

Our flag: Our flag consists of a Sun with a bright
orange background.
The Sun represents positivity in our free environment.
The orange represents optimism and warmth in our society.
The lion represents our bravery and pride.
